About Us

Kainchi Dham Shri Neeb Karori Baba Ashram

The Kainchi Dham Shri Neeb Karori Baba Ashram Varanasi branch has multi storied building to accommodate pilgrims for Kashi Dham, Ganga Bath and other yatras. Currently the guest house has 145 rooms. Facilities include 24 hour utility water, drinking water, dormitory, vegetarian swatwik food in clean and hygienic environment. We can also provide cooking place for the pilgrims travelling in group those are bring their cookers and utensils. The rooms are modern, equipped with elegant furniture, bathroom fittings, western type water closets, curtains etc. Some rooms have air conditioners and water heaters. Pilgrimages are provided accommodation, prasadam, proper guidance for Darshan, easy and smooth performance of rituals, cultural and religious programme to create an atmosphere of high spirituality.
